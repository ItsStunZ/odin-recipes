Ingredients

  • Meat: 1 lb ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork/sausage)
  • Sauce 1 jar (approx. 24-32 oz) marinara sauce, or 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes + 1 small can tomato paste
  • Noodles: 1 box (9-12 sheets) lasagna noodles (oven-ready or standard)
  • Cheese Layer: 15 oz ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese), 2-3 cups shredded mozzarella, and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Aromatics/Seasoning: 1 onion (diced), 2 cloves garlic (minced), Italian seasoning, salt, pepper.
  • Binder: 1 large egg (mixed into ricotta for better texture).

Steps

  1. Meat Sauce: Brown ground beef with diced onion and garlic. Stir in tomato sauce/paste and seasonings; simmer for 10-20 minutes
  2. Cheese Mix: In a bowl, combine ricotta, half the mozzarella, parmesan, egg, and herbs
  3. Assemble: In a 9x13 dish, layer: meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture. Repeat, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375 Deg F (190 Deg C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 10-15 minutes until bubbly.
